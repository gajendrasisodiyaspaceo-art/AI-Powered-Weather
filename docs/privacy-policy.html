Privacy Policy

MausamChef

Effective Date: April 17, 2026

MausamChef ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI-powered food recommendation application that suggests meals based on weather conditions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose Storage
Email address Account creation and authentication Supabase (cloud)
Password Account authentication Supabase (hashed, never stored in plain text)
Device location (latitude/longitude) Fetch local weather data In-memory only (not stored on servers)
Diet preference Personalize food suggestions Supabase (cloud)
Health goal Personalize food suggestions Supabase (cloud)
Language preference Display content in chosen language Supabase (cloud)
Theme preference Dark/light mode display Supabase (cloud)
Favorite food items Save preferred recommendations Supabase (cloud)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Location data is used solely to fetch current weather conditions from OpenWeatherMap API. Your location is never stored on our servers or shared with advertisers.
  • Preferences (diet, health goal, language) are used to personalize your food recommendations.
  • Email is used only for account authentication and password recovery.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

MausamChef uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Provides authentication and database storage for user accounts and preferences. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • OpenWeatherMap API — Receives your approximate location (latitude/longitude) to return weather data. OpenWeatherMap Privacy Policy
  • Groq API — Our AI service receives weather context and your food preferences (no personal identifying information such as email or location) to generate food suggestions. Groq Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies, ensuring users can only access their own data.
  • Location data is held in-memory only and is not persisted to any server.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the app's Settings screen.
  • Modify your preferences (diet, health goal, language, theme) at any time.
  • Delete your favorites and saved data from within the app.
  • Delete your account — contact us at the email below and we will remove all your data from our systems.

6. Children's Privacy

MausamChef is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Advertising and Tracking

MausamChef does not contain any advertisements. We do not use any analytics tracking, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
