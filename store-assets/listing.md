## Full Description

MausamChef - Weather pe depend karta hai khaana!

Wondering what to eat? Let the weather decide! MausamChef is your AI-powered food companion that suggests the perfect Indian dishes based on your current weather, location, time of day, and personal preferences.

Garmi mein? Get refreshing lassi, curd rice, and watermelon juice suggestions.
Baarish ho rahi hai? Pakode, samosa, and garam chai recommendations!
Thand lag rahi hai? Warm up with rajma chawal, dal makhani, and gajar halwa.

AI-POWERED RECOMMENDATIONS
Our AI chef analyzes your local weather, temperature, humidity, and time of day to suggest 12 perfectly matched food items across 6 categories: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks, Drinks, and Dessert.

MADE FOR INDIA
- Bilingual: Full Hindi + English support
- Regional foods: Suggestions based on your city and state
- Indian cuisine focus: From dosa to biryani, chai to kulfi

PERSONALIZED FOR YOU
- Diet preferences: Veg, Non-Veg, Vegan, or Everything
- Health goals: Weight Loss, Muscle Gain, Balanced
- Save your favorites for quick access
- Share food suggestions with friends and family

LOCATION-BASED
- Automatic weather detection using your location
- Search and switch between cities
- 5-day weather forecast included

BEAUTIFUL DESIGN
- Animated weather cards with Lottie animations
- Dynamic color gradients based on temperature
- Dark mode support
- Smooth animations and haptic feedback

DETAILED FOOD INFO
- Hindi and English names for every dish
- Ingredients list, prep time, and difficulty level
- Calorie information and healthy food badges
- One-tap YouTube recipe search
- Easy sharing with friends

PRIVACY FIRST
- Your location is used only to fetch weather data
- No ads, no tracking
- Your data stays secure

Download MausamChef and never wonder "aaj kya khaayein?" again!
